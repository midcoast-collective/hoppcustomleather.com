Hopp Custom Leather
St. Joseph, MO

Hopp Custom Leather is a one man operation, located in Saint Joseph, MO. Just a guy, his leather… and his dog! Leatherwork is my passion, my creative outlet, and I take it very seriously. I have been blessed with the opportunity to take something I truly enjoy and turn that into a full time career. Guns and leather... That’s a tough combination to beat!

Each piece of gunleather is handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail, by me. When you contact Hopp Custom Leather, you will always be communicating and working with the owner / holster maker / lead designer / janitor, etc… me :) My goal is to make your experience with Hopp Custom Leather a great one! I’m not interested in selling you just a holster… I want to build you a unique piece of craftsmanship that you will be proud to show off, and I will be proud to put my name on. Something you will be excited to get … and I’ll have fun making!!

Thanks for checking out my website. If you would like to place an order, have questions, or just want to talk guns and gear… give me a shout!

Cheers,

Eric B. Hopp

Holsters

Each holster is handmade with the highest quality leather, dyes, and materials available. Hand boned to fit your specific gun, oiled, buffed and sealed with a moisture resistant coat. The edges are burnished to a slick finish. No shortcuts, just quality gunleather made to last… and made to look good!

* Exotic hide availability, as well as leather and thread colors may vary.

IWB/OWB Combo

Sometimes it is tough to pick just one! The Hopp Custom Leather “IWB/OWB Combo” holster gives you the best of both. This holster is designed for strong side carry, at approximately 3-5:30 on your hip. The holster is designed to carry the gun with a 15 - 20 degree forward cant. This helps to maximize the your ability to conceal the firearm. It also aids in a quick, smooth draw and presentation.

This holster can be made with either belt loop snaps or tuckable metal clips for IWB carry. The clips or belt loops can be easily removed for OWB carry.

The belt loops are made of horsehide, with mil-spec “Pull-The-Dot” directional snaps. The basic IWB/OWB Combo comes in your choice of leather and stitch color. Don’t be afraid to get creative!

IWB (Inside the Waistband) Only

When concealing your firearm is the main concern, IWB carry is one of the best options. The Hopp Custom Leather “IWB Only” is made for strong side carry, at approximately 3-5:30 on your hip. The holster is designed to carry the gun with a 15 - 20 degree forward cant. This helps to maximize the your ability to conceal the firearm. It also aids in a quick, smooth draw and presentation.

This holster can be made with either belt loop snaps or tuckable clips for IWB carry. The clips can be adjusted for ride height and cant.

The belt loops are made of horsehide, with mil-spec “Pull-The-Dot” directional snaps. The basic IWB Only comes in your choice of leather and stitch color. Choose from a number of different options to make it your own!

Single Clip IWB Holsters

Need to grab your gun and go? The “IWB Single Clip” holster is a great choice for small to medium firearms. Made with a tuckable metal clip… this holster can be worn strong side IWB. It is also great for carrying in a purse or bag. Easy on, easy off… and excellent for deep concealment.

Pocket Holsters

When a belt holster is not an option, pocket carry is a great choice for smaller semi-autos. This holster is designed to be carried in the front pocket, or a cargo pocket. The shape helps keep the holster in your pocket when drawing your firearm.

Mag Carriers

OWB Single Mag - A matching mag carrier is great compliment to your custom holster! The OWB Single Magazine carrier is made to be worn on your weak side, with zero cant. Keep those extra rounds handy!

OWB Double Mag - Just like the single mag carrier… but twice the fun! The OWB Double Magazine carrier is best suited for carrying two of your favorite single stack magazines.
